Spaghetti

Description

This classic spaghetti recipe features a rich and flavorful tomato sauce, perfectly cooked al dente pasta, and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese. It's a simple yet satisfying dish that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the tomato sauce until well coated.
  5. Serve with a generous sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese.
