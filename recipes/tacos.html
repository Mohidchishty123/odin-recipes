Tacos

Description

These delicious tacos are packed with seasoned ground beef, fresh veggies, and topped with a sprinkle of cheese. They are a perfect combination of flavors and textures that make a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 8 taco shells or soft tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream and salsa for serving

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and water to the skillet. Stir well and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm the taco shells or tortillas according to package instructions.
  4. Fill each taco shell with the seasoned ground beef, then top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and shredded cheddar cheese.
  5. Serve with sour cream and salsa on the side.
